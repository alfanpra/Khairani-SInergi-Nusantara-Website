# PT Khairani Sinergi Nusantara

Corporate website for **PT Khairani Sinergi Nusantara (KSN)** — an energy and industrial services company in Indonesia.

## About This Site

This website introduces KSN to clients and partners. It covers who we are, what we do, our team, and how to reach us.

## Pages

- **Home** — company overview and highlights
- **About** — vision, mission, and values
- **Services** — our core service lines
- **Team** — leadership and key personnel
- **Contact** — phone, email, and office address
